### ☆ 파트 7 레이아웃 핵파악 (Reading Framework)

* **[마무리: 행동 유도]** 좌석이 한정되어 있음을 강조하며, **[등록 마감 기한 고지와 함께 참가 신청용 온라인 포털 주소]**를 제공합니다.

```text
SEOUL — In a definitive move that is set to reshape the landscape of the regional agentic economy, tech pioneer Social Brain AI announced on Friday that it has finalized a binding agreement to acquire Muze AI, a highly specialized automated document generation firm, for an estimated cash transaction valued at $12.5 million.

The strategic acquisition is expected to streamline operational workflows for both organizations. Social Brain AI, recognized for its advanced predictive data modeling and LinkMD knowledge base engine, intends to directly embed Muze AI's proprietary document generation software into its existing enterprise platforms. This infrastructure synthesis will allow the combined entity to aggressively penetrate the lucrative B2G (business-to-government) consulting markets, specifically in the Jeonbuk region where Muze AI already maintains strong institutional ties.

Industry analysts have responded favorably to the announcement. "This transaction is a masterclass in architectural synergy," noted Min-ah Song, a senior technology analyst at the Regional Commerce Institute. "By combining Muze AI's structured database ontology with Social Brain AI's expansive Supabase server resources, they effectively eliminate processing latency and create a powerhouse capable of securing major public sector contracts."

Under the terms of the agreement, Muze AI will operate as a wholly owned subsidiary of Social Brain AI. Hana Tanaka, the founding CEO of Muze AI, will transition into the role of Chief Innovation Officer for the parent company, while Social Brain AI's current Chief Operating Officer, James Albright, will oversee the structural budget and asset integration. The transaction is subject to standard regulatory approvals and is anticipated to close by the end of next month.
```

### ③ 3초 컷 정답 저격 해설 (Commentary)
* **1. 정답: (B) The acquisition of a specialized software firm by a technology pioneer**
  * **단서 매칭 (목적/주제 저격):** 첫 번째 단락에서 핵심 팩트를 스캔합니다. "기술 선두주자인 Social Brain AI가 금요일에 자동 문서 생성 전문 기업인 Muze AI를 1,250만 달러에 인수하기로 하는 구속력 있는 계약을 완료했다고 발표(Social Brain AI announced... it has finalized a binding agreement to acquire Muze AI, a highly specialized automated document generation firm)했다"고 명시하고 있습니다. 따라서 기업 인수 소식을 다룬 (B)가 정답입니다.
* **2. 정답: (B) To integrate automated document generation tools into its platforms**
  * **단서 매칭 (인수 목적 저격):** 두 번째 단락 두 번째 문장을 정확히 타격해야 합니다. 지문에서는 "Social Brain AI는 Muze AI의 독점적인 문서 생성 소프트웨어를 자사의 기존 기업용 플랫폼에 직접 내장할 계획(intends to directly embed Muze AI's proprietary document generation software into its existing enterprise platforms)"이라고 기술하고 있습니다. 지문의 `embed... proprietary document generation software`를 보기 (B)의 `integrate automated document generation tools`(자동 문서 생성 도구 통합)로 매끄럽게 페러프레이징한 선지가 확실한 정답입니다.
* **3. 정답: (C) She will become the Chief Innovation Officer at Social Brain AI.**
  * **단서 매칭 (인물 행보 팩트 체크):** 마지막 단락 두 번째 문장을 정밀 스캔합니다. "Muze AI의 설립자이자 CEO인 타나카 하나 씨는 모회사의 최고혁신책임자(CIO) 직책으로 전환할 예정(Hana Tanaka... will transition into the role of Chief Innovation Officer for the parent company)"이라고 명시되어 있습니다. 여기서 모회사(`parent company`)는 인수를 진행한 Social Brain AI를 가리키므로 보기 (C)가 정확한 팩트 매칭 정답입니다.
